TransferNow – External Account Ownership Verification

Flow #1: Instant Verification - Success

  • On the Add Account page enter "000000000" as the routing number.
  • Enter any account number except ‘111111111’ or leave blank.

Flow #2: Real Time Verification (No MFA) - Success

  • On the Add Account page enter "111111111" as the routing number.
  • Enter “000000000” in Account Number.

Flow #3: Real Time Verification (MFA) - Success

  • On the Add Account page enter "111111111" as the routing number.
  • Enter “111111111” in Account Number (or leave blank).

Flow #4: Trial Deposit Initiation

  • On the Add Account page enter "111111111" as the routing number.
  • Enter any account number.
  • Please Click “Verify with bank deposits“ button for TD Initiation(trial deposit flows).
  • Click on the “Send me two deposits” button.
  • Click on the “Done” button.

Flow #5: Trial Deposit Verification

  • Click on the ‘Click here to verify’ link in the Alert on the TN home page to activate TD deposit verification.
  • Click “Verify” for the Suntrust, Checking, xxxx123 account from My Other Accounts.
  • Enter 0.0 for both amounts = Failure Message.
  • Enter any other number = Success.
