What do the Different Statuses Mean?

Pending

Transactions are marked Pending from the time the transfer is requested until the transfer is released for processing. After the transaction is released for processing, the status changes to In-Process.

In-Process

Transactions are marked In-Process from the time the transfers is released for processing until the transfer between financial institutions is complete. After the funds have been transferred, the status changes to Complete.

Completed

Transactions are marked Completed after the transfer between financial institutions is complete.

Canceled

Transactions are marked Canceled as soon as you or the system cancels a transfer.

Rejected

Transactions are marked Rejected when the debit for an account can’t be processed successfully.

Stopped

Transactions are marked Stopped as soon as you or [Host FI Name] Customer Service stops the transfer. This applies only when you are sending money to other people.

Declined

Transactions are marked Declined when the transfer is canceled by TD Bank Customer Service. This is typically performed as a fraud/risk prevention measure.

Failed

Transaction are marked Failed for reasons like the account is closed, or the account was not found at the receiving institution.

On-Hold

The transfer was placed on hold because identity verification either failed, or was not completed when the transfer was created. Please call 1-800-493-7562 to remove the hold.
